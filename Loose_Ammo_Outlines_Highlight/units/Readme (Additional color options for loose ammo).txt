There are additional folders included, that can be used to change what contour effect gets applied.
To change them, just rename any folder that has "(TEXT) pickup", to "pickup."
i.e.
If you want to use HOT&COLD colors, rename "(Hot&Cold) pickups" to pickups. 

Listed out

White:
You will see a white contour, regardless of the distance, except when you are on a different floor.

HOT & COLD:
Contour outline changes to blue, when you are far from it, and red when you get close.

White when close:
You will only see the white contour when you are close to ammo drops.

White when far:
You will see the white contour through the walls, and disappears when you are close to it (similar to teammate contours)
